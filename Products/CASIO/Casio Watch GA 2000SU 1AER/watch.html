Casio Watch GA-2000SU-1AER

$129.00

Casio G-Shock GA-2000SU-1AER is a trendy Unisex watch. Case material is Plastic/Resin while the dial colour is MultiColour. The features of the watch include (among others) a chronograph as well as an alarm. In regards to the water resistance, the watch has got a resistancy up to 200 metres. This means it can be used for professional marine activity, skin diving and high impact water sports, but not deep sea or mixed gas diving. The watch is shipped with an original box and a guarantee from the manufacturer.
